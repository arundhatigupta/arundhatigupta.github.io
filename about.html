About Me

Hey there!

With 5+ years of overall experience, I am a Software Engineer II at Uber, where I leverage my expertise in mobile development to design and build seamless user experiences that strengthen trust and combat fraud.

A graduate in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Patna, I love to combine technical excellence with a deep commitment to mentorship and leadership. I am a Women Techmakers Ambassador, IAmRemarkable Workshop Facilitator, and a Mentor, where I actively support and empower women in technology. At Uber, I also represent the Women at Uber ERG as the India Lead, helping shape a culture of inclusion and representation.

My speaking journey reflects my passion for sharing knowledge and inspiring others - I have spoken at the global Grace Hopper Celebration (GHC) 2023 and GHC India 2024, where I shared my perspectives on building impactful technology and creating equitable opportunities for everyone.

Outside of work, I like creative writing and have a passion for fitness, believing that resilience in personal life translates to resilience in professional journeys.

If this resonates with you and if you would like to connect for a meaningful collaboration, please feel free to get in touch!
